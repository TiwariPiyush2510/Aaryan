About Aaryan

Aaryan Tiwari is an Indian singer, songwriter, and composer based in Mumbai. Born and raised in Indore, Aaryan discovered his passion for music while pursuing engineering at IIT Roorkee. He has collaborated with legendary singer Arijit Singh and filmmaker Mahesh Bhatt, making his mark in Bollywood with hits like "Zaroorat" and "Looteri".
