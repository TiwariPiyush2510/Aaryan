About Aaryan

Aaryan Tiwari is an Indian singer, songwriter, and composer based in Mumbai. Born and raised in Indore, Aaryan discovered his passion for music while pursuing engineering at the prestigious IIT Roorkee.

After graduation, instead of pursuing further studies, Aaryan followed his heart into music. He moved to Dubai where he set up a small studio to hone his craft. His talent caught the attention of renowned filmmaker Mahesh Bhatt, who gave him his first break in the telefilm Naamkarann, where Aaryan composed, wrote, and sang "Chal Meri Jaan."

Aaryan has collaborated with legendary singer Arijit Singh on the recreation of "Aaj Jane Ki Zid Na Karo" and made his Bollywood debut with "Looteri" in Happy Hardy and Heer (2020) featuring Himesh Reshammiya. His notable works include "Zaroorat" (Zee Music), "Mujhe Aur Kya Chahiye," and his latest releases.

Biography

Aaryan Tiwari is an Indian singer, songwriter, and composer based in Mumbai, who has carved a unique path from the halls of IIT Roorkee to the stages of Bollywood. Born and raised in Indore, Aaryan's journey represents the perfect blend of engineering precision and artistic passion.

While pursuing his engineering degree at IIT Roorkee, Aaryan discovered his true calling in music. After graduation, he relocated to Dubai where he established a personal studio to hone his craft. His breakthrough came through social media when renowned filmmaker Mahesh Bhatt discovered his talent, leading to his first major opportunity composing and singing for the telefilm Naamkarann.

His versatility is evident in collaborations with industry giants like Arijit Singh on a legendary track recreation and Himesh Reshammiya on the film Happy Hardy and Heer. Known for his distinctive mouth trumpet technique, Aaryan brings a unique sonic signature to his performances.
